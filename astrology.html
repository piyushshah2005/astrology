Why Celestia?

Astrology is one of humanity's oldest frameworks for self-understanding — a language that maps the inner world onto the outer cosmos. At Celestia, we take it seriously. Not as a party trick or a meme, but as a nuanced, symbolic system that rewards deep study.

We founded Celestia in 2019 with a simple conviction: that astrology is most powerful when it's specific, honest, and delivered without mystical fog. Every reading we give is grounded in classical technique, communicated in plain language, and tailored to where you actually are in life.

Our Approach

We draw primarily from Hellenistic and Medieval astrological traditions — older, more structured methods that treat astrology as a craft rather than a vibe. We layer in modern psychological insights where they genuinely add depth.

We don't believe your Sun sign is your destiny. We believe your birth chart is a rich, layered portrait — and understanding even a small part of it more clearly can change how you move through the world.

About Astrology

Is astrology actually real? What's it based on? +
Astrology is a symbolic language — a system for meaning-making that maps celestial positions onto human experience. Whether it's "real" in a scientific sense depends on your framework. What we do know is that millions of people across thousands of years have found it deeply useful for self-reflection, timing decisions, and understanding personality. We take it seriously as a craft, not a magic formula.
What's the difference between Western and Vedic astrology? +
Western astrology is based on the tropical zodiac (tied to Earth's seasons), while Vedic (Jyotish) astrology uses the sidereal zodiac (tied to fixed star positions). They also differ in techniques, emphasis, and purpose. At Celestia, we practice Western astrology rooted in Hellenistic traditions, though we respect Vedic work deeply and can recommend practitioners if that's what you're seeking.
Is my Sun sign really the most important thing in my chart? +
Not necessarily. Your Sun sign reflects your core identity and life purpose, but your Rising sign (Ascendant) shapes how you appear and how you navigate the world — and many astrologers argue it's equally or more important. Your Moon sign governs your emotional inner world. A full birth chart reading considers all of these and far more.

Booking & Readings

What do I need to book a birth chart reading? +
You'll need your date of birth, exact time of birth (even an approximate time helps, but the more precise the better), and your place of birth. The birth time is especially important for calculating your Rising sign and the house positions in your chart. If you don't know your birth time, we can still do a solar chart reading — just let us know.
How long does a reading take? +
Standard readings run 60–75 minutes via video call. We send you a recording afterward so you can revisit the insights at your own pace. Deep-dive readings (birth chart + transits + solar return) can run 90 minutes. Written reports are also available if you prefer reading over talking.
Do you offer readings for couples? +
Yes — our Synastry reading compares two birth charts to reveal the dynamics, strengths, and friction points between two people. This works for romantic partners, close friends, or business partners. Both people should ideally be present for the call, and we'll need birth data for both.
What's your cancellation and rescheduling policy? +
You can reschedule up to 24 hours before your appointment with no charge. Cancellations made with less than 24 hours' notice are non-refundable, though we'll offer you a credit toward a future booking. Life happens — just let us know as early as you can.

Getting Started

I'm completely new to astrology. Where should I begin? +
Start with a birth chart reading — it's the foundation of everything. You'll walk away with an understanding of your Sun, Moon, and Rising signs, your chart's dominant themes, and an introduction to the twelve houses. From there, you'll have enough grounding to explore further on your own or book follow-up sessions.
Can you teach me how to read my own chart? +
Absolutely. We offer educational readings specifically designed to walk you through your chart step by step, explaining the technique as we go. It takes a bit longer but leaves you with real skills. Mention this when you book, and we'll tailor the session accordingly.
Do you offer gift readings? +
Yes! Gift readings are available for all of our services. Reach out via the Contact page and we'll send you a gift certificate you can pass along. The recipient books their own appointment at a time that suits them.
